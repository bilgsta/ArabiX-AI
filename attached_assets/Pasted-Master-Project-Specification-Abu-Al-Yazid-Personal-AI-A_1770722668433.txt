Vision

Build a secure, production-ready, Arabic-first AI platform that competes with ChatGPT and Google Gemini while maintaining a unique Arabic identity in design, language, privacy, and user experience.

The platform must be visually polished, technically robust, scalable, and trustworthy for users in the Arab world and beyond.

Core Purpose

Abu Al-Yazid is a smart personal AI assistant designed to support users with:

Intelligent AI conversations

Content writing and generation

Programming and development help

Education and learning assistance

Business, marketing, and research

Image, video, document, and voice analysis

The platform must support Modern Standard Arabic, Arabic dialects, and multiple global languages.
